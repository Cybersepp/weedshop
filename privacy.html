PRIVACY

What we keep, and why

Woody Weedpecker is a small, static site. We don't run analytics, we don't track you, and we don't sell anything online. Here's what does get stored when you visit — kept short and honest.

Functional storage

Your browser holds two small items for us — your language preference (wwp-lang) and your age gate answer (wwp-age-confirmed). They stay on your device, and clearing your site data removes them. We never see them.

Third-party content

A few third-party resources are embedded — Google Maps in Find Us, Google Fonts for the typeface, and public CDNs for the homepage animations. Those services may set their own cookies or log your IP, which is between you and them.

Your rights under Thailand's PDPA

Under the Personal Data Protection Act you can ask us about, correct, or delete any personal data we hold on you. This site doesn't keep customer records, but if you've messaged us through Instagram, WhatsApp, or email, reach out the same way and we'll handle it.
