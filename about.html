ABOUT US

The Woody Weedpecker Story

Woody Weedpecker is not just a cannabis shop. It's a community, a culture, and a carefully assembled collection of the finest cannabis strains from across Thailand.

In a short time our brand has become one of the most talked-about and recognizable names on the island — thanks to one guiding principle: quality always comes first.

We work with Thailand's top farms and professional cannabis companies that grow to modern standards. Our partners include farms using organic and living-soil methods, medical companies, and winners and competitors of the largest cannabis cup competitions.

Every strain goes through careful selection before it reaches our shelves. We pay attention not only to THC, but also to flavor, terpene profile, product purity, proper drying, curing, and the overall quality of the effect. It matters to us that a product is not only potent, but genuinely clean, safe, and enjoyable.

Woody Weedpecker is a brand that brings people together from all over the world. In just six months we have built a large international community, gathered a loyal audience, and proven that quality service, an honest approach, and a love for the culture are truly valued.

But Woody isn't only about cannabis.

We create an atmosphere. We host events, tastings, themed nights, gaming tournaments, movie nights, cannabis sommelier evenings, and other activities where people can meet, connect, and become part of our community.

Our shop is a place people come to not just for a product, but for the emotions, the atmosphere, and the people.

Woody Weedpecker keeps growing every day. We develop the brand, build a strong community, search for the best strains, and create a new level of cannabis culture in Thailand.
