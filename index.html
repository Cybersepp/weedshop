FUN FACTS

Did you know?

Cannabis contains over 100 distinct cannabinoids — THC and CBD are just the two most famous.

Terpenes — the same compounds that scent pine, citrus, and lavender — shape how a strain actually feels.

Sativa, indica, hybrid is the classic shorthand. Most modern strains are crossbreeds — the lineage matters more than the label.

A slow cure — drying flower in sealed jars for weeks — can change the smoke more than the strain itself.

Thai cannabis culture runs deep. Long before legalization, it was a quiet staple in northern Thai kitchens.
